Build an icon of the Star Wars universe

Inspire youngsters and adults with this 75257 LEGO® Star Wars™ Millennium Falcon model. This brick-built version of the iconic Corellian freighter features an array of details, like rotating top and bottom gun turrets, 2 spring-loaded shooters, a lowering ramp and an opening cockpit with space for 2 minifigures.
